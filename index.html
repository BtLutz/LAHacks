Choosing an Event

Difficulty Description
Learning Learning events are perfect for the beginner hackathon attendee. Shoot to learn coding launguages, meet friends, and collect swag; repeat.
Competitive Competitive events take the principles of a learning hackathon to a higher level, with more serious individuals trying to learn how to create a project rather than learn a language
Division 1 Division 1 events raise the bar from competitive events high as possible. These events bring talent from across the nation, resulting in massive amounts of hacking. Bring a camera
Choosing the right hackathon to attend is key to your success as a hacker.

Your Expectations

Expect the unexpected. Meet new people. Always meet new people. meet more new people. Stop for a snack. Learn something about coding that you didn't previously know. Strive to make something awesome. Team up with people you like, and try to pick people smarter than thou. Finally, have fun. It's what these events are all about.
